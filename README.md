# ChildGuard (Educational Support & Child Labor Prevention Platform)

**ChildGuard** is a web-based platform designed to reduce poverty by enabling educational support for children and preventing child labor. It connects parents, sponsors, volunteers, and NGOs to facilitate sponsorships and protect children’s rights through awareness and reporting mechanisms.

---

## Features

- **Educational Sponsorship:** Supports school fees, books, and uniforms for underprivileged children.
- **Anonymous Reporting:** Allows reporting of child labor incidents safely and anonymously.
- **Awareness Hub:** Educates users about child rights and labor laws.
- **User Roles:** Parent, Sponsor, Volunteer, NGO/Moderator for applying, verifying, sponsoring, and tracking support.
- **Real-time Updates:** Notifications for application statuses, sponsorships, and child progress.

## Objectives

1. Facilitate educational sponsorships to reduce poverty.
2. Prevent child labor through anonymous reporting.
3. Raise awareness about child rights and labor laws.
4. Ensure transparent application and verification processes.
5. Keep sponsors and parents updated on child progress and sponsorship status.
